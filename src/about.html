SHL Builders

General Contracting & Construction Management

The owners of SHL BUILDERS gained construction experience from the bottom and worked their way up. They know all the processes inside out and what helps them to manage each step of the project to get a fantastic result of satisfied clients.

Our Mission

To become a go-to contractors’ company for high end services within the interior renovation industry by providing the best quality of materials, services and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Professionalism

We know how important the endurance of home renovation is. That’s why we are promising you only professional strategies and services to provide a long-lasting effect. Our 10-year experience influences our values in the way that requires us to maintain the highest quality measures to be executed for each client.

Reliability

We have heard of fellow contractors who leave the job half way through after they got their last payment. SHL Builders is not like that. We understand the frustration each client feels when the renovation process begins and continues. As a company our mission is to minimize that frustration and assure the client of our trustworthiness. In simpler words, we do not abandon our projects, we stay with the client until the end and make sure that expectations are exceeded.

Quality

We want to be responsible for the marvels of your home space. This could be achieved only through a highly detailed process of selecting the best quality materials and resources for the job. That's why we are very picky about who we take on as subcontractors and also why we have brought satisfaction to all our clients.

Feedback

We encourage customers to leave comments and reviews they had about our services on our platforms. This way we are able to track our progress and display our works to potential clients and are aware of the aspects that you would want us to improve on.

Customer Satisfaction

Our company was based on the notion of being a support system to our clients and helping them bring to life that homey and beautiful place they always wanted to live in. It is in our interests to satisfy the customer. We want to build up the trust and ensure a harmonious coworking atmosphere during the project and afterwards. As a thank you to our customers, we also provide courteous touch up services for a year after completion.

Flexibility

Let’s face it: the renovation of any home is a very expensive and challenging process and sometimes you need to change things up a bit financially to accommodate your needs and we absolutely understand that. We will always work around your available budget and find an optimal solution.

Our Values

The company started with 2 brothers who always had a passion for the industry. After more than 7 years of valuable and intense work, they began their own company to combine the most effective methods of construction in order to satisfy each customer’s needs and wants.

Their crucial knowledge and skills helped them create a strong network with professional and, most importantly, reliable vendors who supply SHL Builders with the necessary materials and services of the highest quality on the market. As a company, we are like nobody else understand the value of time, and established the project's time frame to avoid any extra delays during renovation of your home.
