Insurance

General Liability and Umbrella Insurance: Our policy is covering up to $10,000,000 of general liability and umbrella insurance.

Our policy not only meets the requirements but also goes over the amount set by the NYC and NYS institutions. It covers all our employees working on the site during each project. Another part of our insurance policy is coverage of auto liability which is $1,000,000. Same amount is provided for the workers compensation insurance. For a full security of our workers, we have acquired disability insurance for every one of our professionals.
