How we work?

All-In-One Approach to Home Renovations.

In-person Estimate

It gives us a more accurate perspective on the scope of work and the measurements of the entire space to create a detailed and more accurate estimate.

Specifications Development

Preparing an estimate and cost analysis for the client. This way we adhere to all the requests made and list the materials and resources necessary to make the project the way the client planned.

Ongoing Communications

The conversation with the client doesn’t stop after the first proposal. We make sure to put the customer into the know of every decision, progress made on the customer’s grounds during the process of renovation.

Project Management

This goes hand in hand with ongoing communication because we keep in touch with the clients and interior designers about the developments happening on the site. Our project managers monitor the process from A to Z and create reports to establish an understanding among all involved parties.

Vendors Selection

SHL carefully selects subcontractors for each task to make sure our high-quality standards align with their ideology of excellent customer service and professionalism. We review the bids they submit paying close attention to the source of the materials and then select only the best proposition.

Courteous Care

We provide a year-long support service to both commercial and residential clients. To ensure the result that will withstand the test of time, it is crucial to take care of the property after it was renovated.
